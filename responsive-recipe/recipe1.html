Traditional Style ONION PURE made at home

When vampires are rampant, perhaps you want to look at new options for defending yourself. Vampires hate onions so might as well start here with enough stock to last you your lifetime

This is a great treat for many people such as onion lovers, vampire haters, and those who believe onions shouldn't be so layered. It's also great as an ingredient in other recipes but we won't go into that here.

Step 1: Process the onion

Prepare onions as explained above

Pulse the onion in a food processor for 10 to 15 seconds until finely chopped, making sure to scrape down the sides of the bowl.

Step 2: Reprocess the onion

Sometimes finely chopped isn't chopped fine enough.

Pulse the onion in a food processor again for another 30 to 60 seconds until extremely finely chopped, making sure to scrape down the sides of the bowl again to get all the onion bits.

Step 3: Repeat step 2 as needed

Maybe you feel that wasn't enough (I agree), so feel free to repeat step 2 as many times as needed to get desired consistencty

Step 4: Cry

If you haven't cried yet, you should. This isn't a good recipe and you just wasted a bunch of onions, not to mention your eyes are probably burning

Step 5: Repeat step 2 yet again
