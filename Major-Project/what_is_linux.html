What is Linux?

Linux is a free and

open source
Programs where anyone can view the code of, modify the code of or use.
operating system or to put it simply, an alternative to Windows, Mac or Chrome OS. Linux a free alternative to these others on your computer and can provide a different computing experience than these other options may offer.

There are a variety of reasons why you may wish to use Linux over any of the prior versions mentioned. For example, maybe your current operating system is outdated, or you wish to expand what limited software it can run, or maybe you dislike certain aspects like artificial intelligence being baked into your operating system. Another operating system, like Linux, can solve those problems, keep your computer up to date, and even make old computers run like new.
