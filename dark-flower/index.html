Rhododendron

Rhododendron Macrophyllums, AKA Rhododendron Californium, Pacific Rhododendrons, Rhododendrons or just Rhodies, are a plant native to the pacific coast from California to British Columbia [1]. They live along the coast up to 6,000 ft elevation (if you ever so dare wish to escape them) [2].

Soil

  • Drains well [2]
  • Acidic [2]
  • Low nitrogen [2]

Preferable environment

  • Heavy forests [3]
  • Shaded [3]
  • Little water [4]

Dimensions

Rhodies grow between 5 feet and 25 feet depending on sunlight, prefering shade for their maximum growth. [2]
